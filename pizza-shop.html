FIRE STONE PIZZA

Satisfy your hunger with authentic wood-fired pizza at Fire Stone Pizza, our popular pizzeria featuring traditional Italian cuisine with a wild twist. Our stone oven, fired with natural wood, creates the perfect crispy crust and smoky flavor that makes every pizza a memorable experience.

Our menu features classic favorites like Margherita and Pepperoni, as well as unique zoo-inspired creations such as the "Wild Mushroom Safari" and "Carnivore's Delight." We use only the freshest ingredients, including locally sourced vegetables, premium cheeses, and house-made sauces. Our double crust option provides extra room for double the toppings, perfect for sharing with the whole family.

The restaurant features both indoor seating and a spacious outdoor patio where families can dine while enjoying views of the zoo grounds. Kids will love our mini pizzas and animal-shaped garlic bread, while adults can enjoy our selection of Italian sodas and local craft beverages. Fast, friendly service ensures you'll be back to exploring the zoo in no time!
